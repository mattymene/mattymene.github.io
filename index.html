    spottedPD

    spottedPD is a social networking app designed to enhance the social life of students in Padua. Through the app, you can organize events, meet new people, and create lasting memories. Whether it’s organizing an aperitivo, attending a concert, or looking for the crush you saw at a party, spottedPD will help you find new adventure companions to share experiences with.
