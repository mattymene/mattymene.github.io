1. Introduction

Welcome to JoinIn (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application, particularly in relation to Firebase services.

2. Data Collected

We may collect the following types of data through Firebase:

  • Personal Data:
    • Email
    • Full Name
    • Nationality
    • Student Type
    • Username
    • Image
    • Token
    • University
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the app, including logs and analytics.
  • Device Information: Such as device type, operating system, and unique identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use Firebase services for:

  • Authentication: To manage user login and access.
  • Analytics: To improve the performance and user experience of our application.
  • Cloud Firestore & Realtime Database: To store and manage app data securely.
  • Cloud Messaging: To send notifications and updates.
  • Crashlytics: To identify and fix application errors efficiently.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties. However, we may share data in the following circumstances:

  • With Firebase (Google) as a third-party service provider under their privacy policies.
  • When required by law enforcement or regulatory authorities.
  • If necessary to protect our legal rights and comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data. Firebase provides built-in security mechanisms, including encryption and access control policies.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of analytics and data tracking features.
  • Request data portability.
