Introduction

This policy establishes the standards and measures adopted to prevent, identify, and promptly respond to any form of abuse or sexual exploitation of minors on our platform. Our company is committed to ensuring a safe and protected environment for all users, especially minors. This page outlines the methods for reporting inappropriate content or behavior and the actions we will take in response.

Reporting Inappropriate Content and Abuse

If you encounter any content or behavior that may constitute abuse or sexual exploitation of minors, please report it immediately.

How to Report

  • Send an email to: menegalep3@gmail.com
  • Provide a detailed description of the incident, including any screenshots or evidence that might help in the assessment.
  • Indicate the date and time when the incident occurred, if possible.

Measures Taken

Once a report is received, our security team will proceed as follows:

  • Immediate and thorough verification of the reported content.
  • Prompt removal of inappropriate content from the platform.
  • Blocking or removal of accounts responsible for posting the reported content.
  • Reporting to the appropriate authorities for further legal action, if necessary.

These measures are adopted to ensure maximum protection for minors and to maintain our platform as a safe and respectful environment for everyone.

Contact

For further information or to submit a report, please contact us via email at: menegalep3@gmail.com

We encourage you to provide as much information as possible in your report so that our team can intervene promptly and effectively.
