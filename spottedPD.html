    About Us

    iOS

    beta available

    Android

    beta available

    spottedPD is a social networking app designed to enhance the social life of students in Padua. Through the app, you can organize events, meet new people, and create lasting memories. Whether it’s organizing an aperitivo, attending a concert, or looking for the crush you saw at a party, spottedPD will help you find new adventure companions to share experiences with.

    Join Us!

    We are looking for new members to join our team.
    spottedPD is a social network app for Android and iOS, developed in Flutter and currently available in Beta.
    With the goal of officially launching the product on the market and participating in the Start Cup Veneto, we are looking for passionate students like us to complete the team.

    Here are the roles we are looking for:

    UI/UX Designers

    - Role: Help us design intuitive and engaging user interfaces.
    - Skills: basic knowledge of Sketch, Figma, or similar tools

    Flutter Developers

    - Role: Help us complete the development of the mobile application for Android and iOS platforms.
    - Skills: basic knowledge of Flutter

    Marketing Strategists

    - Role: Help us develop marketing strategies to increase user acquisition and engagement.

    If you don't feel perfectly suited for the role, don't be discouraged: we believe that a strong passion and the desire to learn outweigh the knowledge. If you are interested, or want to clarify some doubts, contact us here and we will be ready to answer your questions. We strongly believe in networking and are sure that meeting us will be a great opportunity for both of us!
